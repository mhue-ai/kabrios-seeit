What the product experience is meant to feel like

01

Choose the framework mix that applies

Start with SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, NIST-based programs, customer requirements, or internal control structures that actually matter to the business.

02

Map controls and collect evidence with context

Bring evidence into one system with ownership, timestamps, and review structure so records can support operations, audits, and recurring reuse.

03

See changes that affect readiness

Review signals continuously so teams know what changed, what needs attention, and what affects current posture.

04

Assign remediation and track closure

Turn findings, incidents, exceptions, and gaps into owned work with due dates, accountability, and evidence-backed closure.

05

Extend the same model into adjacent GRC work

Use the same coordination layer for audits, policy attestations, third-party assessments, issue handling, resilience tasks, and risk follow-up.

06

Report to leadership with confidence

Roll the program into clear status summaries for executives, auditors, customers, and boards without rebuilding the story from scratch every cycle.
